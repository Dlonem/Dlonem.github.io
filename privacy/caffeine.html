Privacy Policy — Caffeine Break Buddy

Effective date: July 5, 2026  ·  Last updated: July 14, 2026
Publisher: Dlonem LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”). Contact: officialdlonem@gmail.com
The short version. Caffeine Break Buddy is a personal caffeine-reset and habit-tracking app for Android and iOS. Everything you log — your reset, intake calendar, daily check-ins, energy & sleep ratings, symptoms, badges, and the Caffeine & Sleep estimates — is stored only on your device. We run no servers and cannot see your data. The one exception is advertising: the free version shows ads through Google AdMob, which uses limited device and advertising identifiers to serve them. The one-time premium purchase removes ads. The app is an educational wellness tool — not a medical, diagnostic, or treatment service.

1. Who this applies to

This policy covers the Caffeine Break Buddy mobile application published by Dlonem LLC on Google Play and the Apple App Store. The app is intended for a general adult audience. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13 (or the minimum age required by your region and the platform).

2. What the app stores, and where

Caffeine Break Buddy is a local-first app. The information you enter is stored on your device only. Dlonem LLC operates no servers, no database, and no cloud storage of its own, and therefore has no access to this information and does not transmit it to us.

  • What you can enter. Your onboarding profile (typical caffeine intake and delivery methods, timing, goal, and optional metabolism factors such as age range, sensitivity, smoking status, combined-pill / pregnancy status, and whether you take certain medications), your intake calendar, daily check-ins (energy, mood, headache, focus, sleep quality, cravings), milestone badges, and your bedtime/wake settings. This is used only to power the app's estimates and coaching on your device.
  • No account, no cloud. The app has no sign-in and no server. Your data stays in this app's local storage on your device — we never receive, read, or store it, and it is not synced to any cloud by us.
  • Optional manual backup. You can export a copy of your own data from within the app (Settings → Back up my data) and save it wherever you choose using your device's own share options, then import it later to restore. This is entirely manual and optional; the exported copy goes only where you send it. (On Android, the system's own Auto Backup may separately back up app data to your personal Google Drive under Google's terms — a platform feature we do not control or receive.)
  • Deleting your data. You can reset your current break or erase everything from within the app (Settings → Reset this break / Start over). Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device.

3. Advertising (free version) — Google AdMob

The free version displays ads via Google AdMob (the Google Mobile Ads SDK). To serve and measure ads, AdMob may collect and process data such as your device's advertising identifier, IP address, general device information, and ad interactions, for advertising, analytics, and fraud-prevention purposes. Google handles this data under its own privacy policy. It is encrypted in transit and is kept separate from the personal reset data in Section 2 — AdMob does not receive your check-ins, symptoms, calendar, or profile answers.

  • Google's handling of this data: policies.google.com/privacy and how Google uses information from partner apps.
  • Android: you can reset or delete your advertising ID in Settings → Privacy → Ads.
  • iOS — App Tracking Transparency (ATT): on Apple devices the app will ask for your permission before any tracking that uses your device's advertising identifier (IDFA). If you decline, ads may still be shown but will be non-personalized. You can change this any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Region consent (EU/UK and similar): where required, you will be asked for ad-consent choices through Google's consent mechanism before personalized ads are shown.
  • Removing ads: the one-time premium purchase removes all ads, after which the ad SDK no longer serves ads in the app.

5. Purchases

The optional one-time premium purchase is processed by the platform's billing system — Google Play Billing on Android and Apple In-App Purchase (StoreKit) on iOS. We do not receive or store your payment-card details; the store handles the transaction under its own terms. A Restore Purchases option lets you re-unlock premium on a new device using the same store account.

6. What we do not do

To be explicit: Dlonem LLC does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information; does not build advertising or marketing profiles from your reset data, check-ins, symptoms, or health-related answers; does not require an account, email, or sign-up to use the app; and operates no server or database that receives your tracking data. The only information that leaves your device is the limited advertising data handled by Google (Section 3) and, only if you choose it, an optional backup to your own personal cloud account.

7. Not medical advice

Caffeine Break Buddy is an educational wellness and habit-tracking tool. Its recovery timelines, half-life and sleep-cutoff estimates, and coaching are informational only and are not medical advice, and do not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. Estimates are approximations, not guarantees. Consult a qualified professional for medical advice, especially if you have a heart condition, are pregnant, or take medications.

8. Children

The app is not directed to children and we collect no personal information from anyone — including children — because the app collects no data and transmits nothing to us. There is therefore nothing on our end for us to access or delete. Any data a user enters lives only on their own device and can be erased at any time within the app (Settings → Start over) or by uninstalling.

9. Your choices & rights

Because your personal reset data stays on your device, you control it directly — view, edit, or erase it in the app at any time. For rights relating to ad data processed by Google (such as access or deletion requests under the GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA/CPRA), see Google's privacy policy linked in Section 3; you can also exercise ad choices via your device’s ad/tracking settings, or contact us with any privacy question at officialdlonem@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable time.

10. Data security & retention

Because your tracking data never leaves your device (except to a manual backup you choose to export yourself), it is protected by your device’s own operating-system security and any passcode or biometric lock you use. We retain none of it, because we never receive it — it lives only on your device for as long as you keep the app installed and is removed when you erase it in-app or uninstall. Advertising data processed by Google is retained by Google under its own policies. There is no server-side copy for us to lose, breach, or be compelled to disclose.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves or as store requirements change. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, an in-app notice. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.

12. Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns about this policy or your data: officialdlonem@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable time.
