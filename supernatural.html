Core features

What the mod adds

Traits & events

Four unique supernatural traits — vampires, werewolves, hybrids, and hunters — each woven through a rich tapestry of events that can mark you as one of them, or set you hunting them.

Lifestyles

Five tailored lifestyles with seven perk trees guide vampires, werewolves, hybrids, witches, and hunters on their respective journeys.

Mystical men-at-arms

Command supernatural forces — from vampires and werewolves to ethereal bats and relentless skeletons, six unique units to assert your dominance.

Arcane artifacts

Seek out seven magical artifacts, each promising unparalleled power.

Potion crafting

Brew power in a bottle with a dedicated potion-crafting interface — the Witch's Brew, the Love Potion, the Witch's Mutagen, even the legendary Elixir of Life.

A transformed atmosphere

A main menu swathed in red and seventeen loading screens steep the whole game in supernatural ambiance.

Customize your journey

Your arcane, your rules

Extensive game settings let you tailor how the supernatural touches your campaign — whether you want to hunt it, rule with it, or just let it haunt the edges of the map.
