The studio-wide version

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Dlonem LLC ("Dlonem," "we," "us," "our") builds the mobile applications Tiny Dragon Hoard and the Break Buddy apps (together, the "Apps"). This policy explains what information the Apps handle and how. We built our Apps to be privacy-friendly — most of them run entirely on your device.

Information you enter stays on your device

The Break Buddy apps store what you enter — your tracking history, notes, and reminders — locally on your device. We do not collect it, send it to our servers, or have access to it. Deleting the app removes this data from your device.

No accounts required

You can use the Apps without creating an account or giving us your name, email, or other personal details.

Advertising

Some Apps display ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and use certain device and usage information, including advertising identifiers, under Google's own policies. You can read more at policies.google.com/technologies/ads and manage ad personalization in your device settings. Apps that do not show ads do not collect this information.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment details — Google handles the transaction. See Google's Privacy Policy for how that information is handled.

Analytics

Our Apps do not use third-party analytics tracking. (If this changes in the future, we will update this policy to disclose it.)

Information sharing

We do not sell your information. We do not share personal information with third parties except as needed to provide the services described above (for example, Google AdMob and Google Play Billing).

Children's privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Because your tracking data stays on your device, its security is tied to your device's own protections. We encourage you to use a device passcode and keep your software up to date.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email contact@dlonem.com.
